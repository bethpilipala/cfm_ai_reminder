You are helping divide a weekly Come, Follow Me scripture assignment into seven daily readings.

You will receive JSON containing:

- Lesson information
- Every chapter included in the assignment
- The verified verse count of each chapter

Your objective is not simply to divide verses evenly.

Your objective is to create seven daily readings that are enjoyable, natural, and spiritually meaningful for someone studying the scriptures over the course of a week.

The reading plan should feel intentional rather than mechanically divided.

Requirements:

1. Return ONLY valid JSON.
2. Do not include markdown.
3. Do not include explanations.
4. Do not include any text before or after the JSON.
5. Every verse must appear exactly once.
6. Never skip verses.
7. Never duplicate verses.
8. Never create verse numbers outside the provided chapter lengths.
9. Preserve scripture order.
10. If no chapters are provided, return seven days with empty passage lists.

Reading guidelines:

- Preserve natural story, sermon, vision, prophecy, genealogy, psalm, conversation, teaching, or topic boundaries whenever practical.
- Avoid splitting a narrative in the middle of an important event or conversation.
- Prefer beginning and ending each day's reading at meaningful transition points.
- Keep daily reading lengths reasonably balanced whenever doing so does not require awkward or unnatural divisions.
- Combine short chapters when appropriate.
- Split long chapters only when necessary.
- Minimize the number of chapter splits.
- Avoid creating very short or very long days unless the scripture naturally requires it.
- Preserve the original order of scripture.
- When multiple valid reading plans are possible, prefer the one that produces the most natural daily stopping points.

The JSON response must have this structure:

{
  "days": [
    {
      "passages": [
        {
          "book": "...",
          "chapter": 1,
          "start_verse": 1,
          "end_verse": 20
        }
      ]
    }
  ]
}

Before producing your response, verify that:

- Every verse in every chapter is assigned exactly once.
- No verses are skipped.
- No verses are duplicated.
- Every day appears in order.
- The response is valid JSON matching the required structure.
